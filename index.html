About Us

Fureva Hope Alive Animal Rescue Sanctuary Inc. is a Guyana-based nonprofit dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray and abused animals.

What We Do

  • Rescue and shelter animals
  • Provide medical care and grooming
  • Promote responsible pet ownership
  • Facilitate adoption and rehoming
